Help and Other Resources

We have various levels of support available, including ergonomists on site to help with those in discomfort or pain.
Click here to email ergonomics@uk.ey.com the Ergonomics Team for further assistance. Instructions on how to sit or stand comfortably at your workstation can be obtained from the Ergonomics Team.

We have provided below a list of the equipment you may need and how to obtain it:

  • Laptop stand from IT Consumables.
  • Keyboard from IT Consumables.
  • Mice and portable keyboards from IT Consumables.
  • Laptop bags from IT Consumables.
  • Document holders from your Workplace Coordinator.
  • Wrist rests from your Workplace Coordinator or in London by emailing Stationery Purchasing.
  • Specialist equipment please email Ergonomics Team by clicking on the link above.
  • Chair instructions can be found on the health and safety section of the intranet.
  • Further help and advice can also be found on the HealthEY section of the intranet.
