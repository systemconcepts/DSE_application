Question 3

Do you have a suitable bag for transporting your laptop?

Information

Use a backpack/rucksack to distribute the load evenly across the body and cut down strain on your arms. Alternatively, you may prefer to use a wheeled bag/trolley. Laptops bags are available from IT Consumables.

Only carry what you need in your laptop bag to reduce the weight.

Click ‘Continue to next question’ or Click ‘Back’ to exit the questionnaire.

Question 4

Do you feel comfortable when working on your laptop away from the office?

Information

You must report any symptoms that you think may be caused or made worse by using your computer when travelling to the health and safety team.

Symptoms to report include headaches, irritated eyes, difficulties reading screen/documents, and tingling, numbness or discomfort in the back, neck, arms or wrists, hands or fingers, feet or legs.

Please press the ‘Help’ button to email an ergonomist.
