Step by Step

Step 1

Adjust your chair height so that you can use your keyboard and mouse with your wrists straight, forearms horizontal and shoulders relaxed. If your feet are not fully supported, use a footrest.

Step 2

Adjust the backrest so that the thickest part of the support is just above your waist,to support the natural ‘S’ shape curve of your spine.

Step 3

Adjust the backrest angle so that it feels comfortable and supports you in an upright position.

Step 4

Place your keyboard and mouse directly in front of you within easy reach. Your upper arms should be by your sides.

Step 5

Position the monitor in front of you at about an arm’s length away.

Step 6

Adjust the monitor height – the top of the screen should be at or slightly below your eye height.

Step 7

Don’t sit for too long – change your posture every few minutes and take regular micro-breaks away from your workstation.
