Travelling

When travelling for work, many hotels, airports and other public places provide designated areas for laptop use. If you are lucky enough to find one, do use it.

Our advice in this section should help you take a few simple precautions to help improve your comfort and well-being wherever you are working.

Additionally, try to ensure you use a privacy screen when in a public place, that your machine has a security lock and you inform the Health and Safety Team if you are undertaking any activities that are not in standard office locations.
